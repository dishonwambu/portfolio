Who I Am:

 

Hey there

My name’s Dishon Wambua, and since I always get asked, I only marginally prefer my full name, and a lot of people call me Dishon.

Up through my life, From an early age I’ve always been deeply interested in computing wanting to know just how computers worked, why they worked and what else they could do.This interest never left me, only growing more profound and passionate with every new discovery I made. From communicating with an artificial intelligence to seeing the wonders of the Internet for the first time, computers makes me live in a fascinated world with just how much power and mystery they hold.

I’ve always loved to study, back during my High school education; the subjects I chose to study have all helped me to develop both myself and my understanding Physics for example has helped me to understand how certain parts of a computer function, while Computing has given me a greater insight into the business aspects of the computer industry.Chemistry and Physics have both helped to improve my analytical and evaluative skills. Within maths I have been lucky enough to have a teacher who was very interested in computer science. He has been especially helpful, lending me books such as Bach: an Eternal Golden Braid. This has given me an insight into the richness that is in computer science. So far in my high school, I used to spent hours in exploring thy futere of computers and partly reading theologian stories like ugustine, Luther, and Calvin; among many others.My interest in computing has not been restricted to the classroom and college life. Outside of the program, the current books most dear to me include the Harry Potter series, The Lord of the Rings, The Curious Incident of the Dog in the Nighttime,, Ender’s Game… obviously, I’m pressing on!!!

My affinity for computing and problem-solving has lead me to study computer science at Chuka University. I have a post explaining how and why I’m in this field here. Currently, I’m looking for attachment in ICT companies such as Sigma customs company and Techsol Software company and among others like Microsoft, Safaricom limitted. under my belt, along with applications to fill out for a research grant I’ll be working on this Holiday, focusing on AWS application and development.

More important than all of that to me, though, is my journey as a Christian. I started down this path with a simple prayer at age 3; but I didn’t really start living out my faith until age 15, when I was baptized on Easter Sunday. Since then, life’s been a crazy adventure of learning to love and live for Christ, getting to know Him better, and really seeking out His calling for my life. I haven’t fully succeeded in any of these areas, and I know I never will–but growing more and more every day is exciting and absolutely worth it. I thank God for how patient He’s been with me through all of my questions, and I’m forever grateful for His peace and truth in my life.

Thanks so much for stopping by my site! If you’d like to learn more about me or any of the things I’ve talked about, don’t hesitate to shoot me a message – if the length of this page is any indication, I love talking with people, and you’re no exception.
